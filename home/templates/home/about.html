This project is the APICBASE internship assigment 2018. The goals was to create a small Recipe Manager application in Django (Python) in which you can create ingredients and recipes and that allows the calculation of food cost for the recipes.

The administrators of the system can create, update and delete ingredients and recipes, while visitors only have access to the details of the recipes.
The ingredients registered in the system needs to have a base amount and an estimated price related to that.
The estimated cost of one recipe is the sum of the cost of all ingredients. The cost of each ingredient is calculated using the formula (QUANTITY_NEEDED * INGREDIENT_BASE_PRICE) / INGREDIENT_BASE_AMOUNT. The quantity needed of the ingredient always consider the current unit measure of the related ingredient.
